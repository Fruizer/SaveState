# SaveState Brand Guidelines

## Brand Position

SaveState is a local-first Workspace Context Manager that restores complete project environments with a single click.

---

# Brand Values

- Fast
- Reliable
- Minimal
- Student-first
- Professional
- Local-first

---

# Brand Personality

- Calm
- Focused
- Efficient
- Technical
- Clean

---

# Logo

The logo combines a Play symbol with a circular restore arrow to represent launching and resuming work.

Meaning:

▶ Launch

↻ Restore

○ Continuous workflow

# Design Principles

- One primary accent color.
- Prioritize whitespace.
- Dark-first interface.
- Consistent spacing.
- Focus on clarity over decoration.

---

# Brand Promise

Open your workspace in one click and start working immediately.
